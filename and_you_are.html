And You Are?

We had the honor of contributing an art piece to the Lake Shore Center for the Arts for their exhibition titled "The Power of Women and the Sacred Feminine." The work we submitted, which you will find detailed below, aims to articulate the concept that women embody a complexity and depth that transcends the mere sum of their parts. This piece was crafted using a combination of collage techniques, ink, and watercolor, reflecting our dedication to capturing the essence of the exhibition's theme.
